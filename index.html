Fariborz Aref’s Website

I am a sociologist and research methodologist working at the intersection of social inequality, comparative inquiry, computational methods, and mixed-methods research. My work examines how social structures shape inequality, mobility, institutional change, and public policy across time and place.

My research connects sociological theory with empirical evidence, emphasizing research design, transparent methods, and explanation. I am especially interested in how evidence is constructed, interpreted, and translated into public relevance.
